    • SCP-007

      Item #: SCP-007

      Object Class: Safe

      Special Containment Procedures: SCP-007 is to be held in locked glass box within a cell of 2x2x2m.

      Description: SCP-007 is a bottle with unknown liquid. SCP-007 text is always blurry and cannot be read. SCP-007 blurs itself's label and some background with it. Attempts to read with a lasers give uncertain responses and values. Attempts has been made with: lasers, microscope, cameras, human eye, mirrors, SCP-████ and even ███████████████.

      Addendum:

      Experiment 1#

      Date: 01/1█/████

      D-2154 has entered the cell with SCP-007.
      No signs of anomalous effect to the D-class personel.
      Dr. █████: D-2154 read the item before you.
      D-2154 is seen trying to read the item's label
      D-2154: Hey what kind of prank is this? This bottle's label is blurred!
      Note: the bottle blurs itself's label and some space around it. The D-class personel stated wrongly.
