    • SCP-003

      Item #: SCP-003

      Object Class: Safe

      Special Containment Procedures: SCP-003 is to be held in a 1x1x1m cell in total darkness and chained.

      Description: SCP-003 is a humanoid creature. SCP-003 just smiles and that's it. Sometimes when SCP-003 stops smiling there will be a earthquake and SCP-003 will try to escape the facility. SCP-003 is not hostile to anyone or anything, it is like SCP-003 lacks the will to damage anything.
