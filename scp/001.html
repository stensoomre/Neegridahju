    • SCP-001

      Item #: SCP-001

      Object Class: Euclid

      Special Containment Procedures: SCP-001 is to be held in 2x2x2 room in total darkness. Only to be checked by lasers.

      Description: SCP-001 is a humanoid creature. It is also a carnivore, only eating humans. SCP-001 acts docile when confronted by a animal. SCP-001's main way of hunting pray is to lure other humans by mimicing the last human's voice and calling for help. Every human who hears SCP-001 mimiced voice will feel necessity to help. Recorded material of SCP-001 mimiced voice gives the same effect.
